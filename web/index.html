Science

Technical Approach

Problem Statement

Pathologists examine thousands of tissue slides daily to detect cancer. This is time-intensive and prone to inter-observer variability. AI models can pre-screen slides to flag suspicious regions, reducing workload and improving early detection rates.

Transfer Learning

EfficientNetB0 was pre-trained on 1.2M ImageNet images, learning general visual features (edges, textures, shapes). We fine-tune this on histopathology to adapt these features to cell morphology without needing millions of medical images.

Grad-CAM Explainability

Clinical AI must be explainable. Grad-CAM computes the gradient of the predicted class with respect to feature map activations, creating a heatmap that highlights the exact regions (cells) that drove the malignant prediction.
